Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our services and medications

How do you ensure medication authenticity?

We source directly from FDA & WHO approved manufacturers with blockchain verification. Every medicine comes with authenticity certificate and serialized tracking.

What is your delivery timeline?

Same-day delivery in major cities (under 4 hours). Next-day delivery nationwide. Free shipping on orders over $50 with temperature-controlled packaging.

Do you accept prescriptions?

Yes, we accept digital and physical prescriptions. Upload your prescription during checkout or consult with our doctors for e-prescriptions instantly.

What is your return policy?

100% money-back guarantee within 7 days for unopened medicines. Free returns on all orders. Contact support within 24 hours of delivery.

Are generic medicines safe?

Absolutely. All generics meet FDA bioequivalence standards (same active ingredients, dosage, and effectiveness as branded medicines at 70% lower cost).

How secure is my data?

Your data is protected with 256-bit SSL encryption, GDPR compliant storage, and HIPAA standards. We never share your medical information with third parties.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Real-time GPS tracking with ETA updates. Receive SMS notifications at pickup, dispatch, and delivery. Track from our app or website dashboard.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, wallets (Paytm/PhonePe), and COD. 100% secure payments with PCI DSS Level 1 compliance.

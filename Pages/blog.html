Health Questions Answered

Common questions from our readers - answered by medical experts

How can I lower cholesterol without medication?

Focus on soluble fiber (oats, beans), healthy fats (avocados, nuts), and 30 minutes daily exercise. Studies show 10-15% LDL reduction in 3 months.

What's causing my constant fatigue?

Most common: thyroid issues (check TSH), vitamin D/B12 deficiency, sleep apnea, or anemia. Get basic bloodwork - 80% of cases diagnosed this way.

Is intermittent fasting safe for women?

Yes for most, but women need shorter windows (12-14 hrs). Avoid extreme fasting near menstrual cycle. Consult doctor if PCOS/thyroid issues present.

When should I worry about blood pressure?

Above 140/90 consistently = hypertension stage 2. 130-139/80-89 = elevated. Monitor 3x/week, same time daily. Lifestyle changes work for 60% of cases.

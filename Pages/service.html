How fast is medicine delivery?

Medicines are delivered within 2-4 hours across 750+ cities. Express delivery available in metro areas (under 90 minutes).

Are medicines 100% authentic?

Yes! All medicines come from licensed pharmacies with barcode verification, temperature-controlled storage, and pharmacist QC.

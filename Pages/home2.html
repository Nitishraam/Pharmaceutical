Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pharmaceutical solutions

How do I order pharmaceutical products?

+

Healthcare professionals can order through our HCP portal, authorized distributors, or by contacting our medical representatives. Bulk orders available for hospitals and pharmacies.

Are your products FDA approved?

-

All our therapeutic products are FDA approved, EMA certified, and WHO prequalified. We maintain compliance across 150+ countries with full regulatory documentation available.

What is your delivery timeline?

+

Standard delivery: 2-5 business days within country, 5-10 days international. Express shipping available for urgent clinical needs. Temperature-controlled logistics maintained.

Do you offer clinical samples?

+

Free clinical samples available for registered HCPs. Complete our online sample request form with prescription credentials. Limited quantities per practice per quarter.

What payment methods accepted?

+

We accept credit cards, wire transfers, purchase orders from institutions, and healthcare financing programs. Volume discounts available for annual contracts.

Is cold chain storage required?

+

Select biologics require 2-8°C storage. We provide detailed storage guidelines and temperature monitoring devices with every shipment. 24/7 support available.
